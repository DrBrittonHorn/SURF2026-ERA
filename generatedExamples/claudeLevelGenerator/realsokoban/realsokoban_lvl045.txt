I need to analyze the game rules and create a level that works with them.

**Game Analysis:**
- Avatar (A) is the player character
- Boxes (*) need to be moved into holes (o) to transform into boxin (.) and score 1 point
- boxin on ground transforms back to box and loses 1 point
- Goal: Get all boxes into holes (reach 0 boxes remaining)
- Avatar can't walk through walls (w), bounces off boxes
- Holes are safe zones where boxes transform and stay

**Key observations from samples:**
1. Levels have walls (w) forming boundaries and mazes
2. Boxes (*) and holes (o) are distributed throughout
3. The number of boxes typically equals the number of holes
4. Avatar needs a path to push boxes into holes
5. Levels vary in complexity with different spatial arrangements
