I need to analyze this GVGAI game to understand the mechanics:

1. **Goal**: Transform all boxes (*) into boxin (o) by moving them into holes, reaching a score where box count = 0
2. **Mechanics**:
   - Avatar (A) is the player-controlled character
   - Boxes (*) can be pushed by the avatar
   - Boxes in holes become boxin (transformTo stype=boxin scoreChange=1)
   - Boxin on ground transforms back to box (transformTo stype=box scoreChange=-1)
   - Walls (w) block movement
   - Goal: Get all boxes into holes permanently

3. **Strategy**: 
   - Each box needs to be pushed into a hole
   - Once in a hole, it becomes boxin and won't revert if it stays in the hole
   - Need equal or more holes
