LevelDescription
I need to analyze this GVGAI game to understand the mechanics:

1. **Goal**: Transform all boxes (*) into boxin (boxes in holes) by moving them into holes (o)
2. **Mechanics**:
   - Avatar (A) is the player character that can move around
   - Boxes (*) can be pushed by the avatar
   - Holes (o) are targets where boxes transform into boxin
   - When a box lands on a hole, it becomes boxin and scores +1
   - When boxin touches ground (not in hole), it transforms back to box and scores -1
   - Win condition: All boxes must be transformed into boxin (counter = 0)

3. **Strategy for level design**:
   - Place boxes and holes strategically so the player must push boxes into holes
   - Avoid placing boxes directly on holes initially
   - Create a puzzle
