I need to analyze this GVGAI game to understand the mechanics:

1. **Goal**: Get all boxes (*) into holes (o) to transform them into boxin (which gives +1 score)
2. **Mechanics**:
   - Avatar (A) can move around on ground (.)
   - Boxes (*) can be pushed by avatar
   - When a box lands on a hole (o), it transforms to boxin and scores +1
   - When boxin is on ground, it transforms back to box and scores -1
   - Win condition: all boxes are gone (transformed to boxin and staying there)
   - Walls (w) block movement
   - Avatar bounces off boxes

3. **Key insight**: Boxes must end up on holes to win. The challenge is maneuvering boxes onto holes using the avatar.
