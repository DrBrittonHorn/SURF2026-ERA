I need to analyze this GVGAI game to understand the mechanics:

1. **Goal**: Get all boxes (*) into holes (o) to transform them into boxin (.) and clear them
2. **Avatar (A)**: The player character that can move around
3. **Boxes (*)**: Need to be pushed into holes (o)
4. **Holes (o)**: When a box falls into a hole, it transforms to boxin and gives +1 score
5. **Boxin**: When boxin touches ground, it transforms back to box and gives -1 score
6. **Win condition**: All boxes must be cleared (limit=0 for box type)

The strategy is to:
- Place boxes and holes so the player can push boxes into holes
- Ensure boxes stay in holes (don't transform back to box on ground)
- Create a puzzle where all boxes end up
