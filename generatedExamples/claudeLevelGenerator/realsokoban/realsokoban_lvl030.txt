1. **Goal**: Remove all boxes (transform them into boxin by dropping them in holes, then transform boxin back to box results in -1 score, so we need to get all boxes into holes and keep them there)

3. **Constraints**:
   - Walls block movement
   - Avatar bounces off boxes when colliding
