LevelDescription
I need to analyze this GVGAI game to understand the mechanics:

1. **Goal**: Transform all boxes (*) into boxin (by dropping them into holes), reaching 0 boxes to win
2. **Avatar (A)**: The player character that can move around
3. **Boxes (*)**: Need to be pushed into holes (o) to transform into boxin
4. **Holes (o)**: When a box falls into a hole, it transforms to boxin and scores +1
5. **Boxin**: When boxin touches ground, it transforms back to box and scores -1
6. **Walls (w)**: Block movement
7. **Mechanics**: Avatar bounces off boxes, boxes bounce off walls, boxes can be undone if they hit walls

The challenge is to create a level where:
- Boxes can be pushed into holes
- The layout allows strategic pushing without getting
