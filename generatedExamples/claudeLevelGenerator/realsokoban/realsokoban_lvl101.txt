2. **Boxes** (*) need to be moved into **holes** (o) to transform them into boxin (scored as +1)
3. **Boxin** on ground can transform back to box (scored as -1)
4. **Win condition**: All boxes must be in holes (box counter = 0)
5. **Walls** (w) block movement
6. **Floor/ground** (.) are walkable spaces

The challenge is to create a puzzle where:
- The player must navigate the avatar to push boxes into holes
- The layout should be solvable but non-trivial
- There should be approximately equal numbers of boxes and holes
- Walls should create interesting pathways
