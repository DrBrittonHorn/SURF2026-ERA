- **Walls (w)**: Obstacles that block movement
- **Goal**: Get all boxes into holes (reduce box count to 0)

**Key mechanics:**
- Avatar bounces off boxes and walls
- Boxes fall into holes and transform
- If boxin leaves a hole, it reverts to a regular box
- Win condition: All boxes must be in holes
