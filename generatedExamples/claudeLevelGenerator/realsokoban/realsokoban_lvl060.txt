I need to analyze this GVGAI game to understand the mechanics:

1. **Goal**: Transform all boxes (*) into boxin (boxes in holes) by moving them into holes (o)
2. **Mechanics**:
   - Avatar (A) can move around on ground
   - Boxes (*) can be pushed by avatar
   - When a box reaches a hole, it transforms to boxin and scores +1
   - When boxin touches ground, it transforms back to box and scores -1
   - Walls (w) block movement
   - Win condition: All boxes must be in holes (counter of box type = 0)

3. **Strategy for a good level**:
   - Have enough boxes and holes for interesting puzzle solving
   - Arrange holes so boxes can be pushed into them
   - Create a challenging but solvable layout
   - Avoid trivial solutions
