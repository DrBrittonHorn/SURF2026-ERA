LevelDescription
Looking at this GVGAI game, I need to understand the mechanics:

- **Avatar (A)**: Shoots bullets upward
- **Pad (G)**: Target sprites to destroy - win condition is to destroy all pads
- **Breakable (d)**: Can be destroyed by bullets, blocks shots
- **Bouncey (B)**: Bullets bounce off it
- **Box (b)**: Physics objects that can be used to hit pads
- **Walls (w)**: Block movement and bullets

The strategy involves:
1. Using the avatar to shoot and destroy pads directly
2. Using boxes as projectiles to hit pads
3. Bouncey tiles can redirect shots
4. Breakable blocks can block shots but also be destroyed
