LevelDescription
Looking at this game, I need to understand the core mechanics:

1. **Goal**: The avatar must reach the green goal (g) while avoiding trucks and logs
2. **Avatar**: Controlled by player, can stand on logs (water sections) to avoid trucks
3. **Logs**: Move left, spawn from forestDense/forestSparse areas, disappear at edges
4. **Trucks**: Move right/left at different speeds, respawn at edges
5. **Water**: Safe zones where logs appear, player can ride logs
6. **Structure**: Walls block movement, goal is the exit

The levels show:
- Top section: walls, grass, goal, and water spawn areas with logs
- Middle section: grass and walls (safe zone for avatar)
- Bottom section: highway with trucks moving back and forth
- Avatar starts at bottom, must navigate to goal at top

The challenge is timing movements between truck
