In this new level, the avatar will have to navigate through a challenging terrain with obstacles and rewards. The "w" blocks are walls that the avatar cannot pass through, while the "*" blocks can be transformed into "." blocks by stepping on them. The "o" blocks are holes that reward points when a box is placed in them
